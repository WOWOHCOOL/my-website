Wireless Charging FAQ

Technical insights for wholesale buyers

01

Does it support 15W fast charge for both iPhone and Android?

Yes. Our smart IC identifies the protocol. It supports 7.5W for iPhone and up to 15W/30W for compatible Samsung, Google, and other Qi-enabled Android devices.

02

What kind of branding can we do on the charging surface?

We offer UV printing, laser etching, and for glass models, under-glass silk-screening which ensures your logo never wears off.

03

Is FOD (Foreign Object Detection) standard?

Absolutely. Every WOWOHCOOL wireless charger includes FOD to stop power transmission if metal objects (keys, coins) are detected, preventing damage.
