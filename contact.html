Quick Answers

How quickly can I expect a response? +

We respond to all inquiries within 4 business hours. Most RFQs receive a preliminary quote within 24 hours. For urgent requests, we recommend contacting us via WhatsApp for the fastest response.

What information should I prepare for a quote? +

To provide an accurate quote, please include: product type (power bank, GaN charger, wireless charger, or car charger), target quantity, branding needs (logo printing, custom packaging), and target market (for certification requirements). Don't worry if you're not sure — our sales team will guide you through the process.

Can I get samples before placing a bulk order? +

Yes. Free samples are available for qualified buyers — you only pay for shipping. Sample delivery takes approximately 3-7 business days via DHL/UPS/FedEx. We also offer sample customization to match your desired specs before mass production.

What are your shipping terms and delivery times? +

Shipping terms include EXW, FOB Shenzhen, DDP, depending on your preference. Production lead time is typically 25-35 days after order confirmation and deposit. Global shipping via sea (LCL/FCL) or air (DHL/UPS/FedEx) is available with real-time tracking.

What quality certifications do your products have? +

All our products are CE, FCC, and RoHS certified as standard. Depending on product type, we also offer Qi/Qi2 (wireless chargers), UL, UN38.3 (power banks), and E-Mark (car chargers) certifications. We support certification testing for your target market requirements.
