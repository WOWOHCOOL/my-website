OEM/ODM Process

ODM: We develop a completely new product to your specifications —ID design, PCBA, tooling, firmware, certifications. MOQ from 2,000 units, lead time 45-60 days. Our 50+ R&D engineers handle the full development cycle. Explore our OEM/ODM services.

1. Inquiry & product selection —choose from 30+ existing SKUs across power banks, GaN chargers, wireless chargers, and car chargers. 2. Quotation within 24 hours, samples in 3-7 days. 3. ODM projects require 45-60 days from design approval to first shipment. Quality Control

Order samples first. Sample turnaround: 3-7 days for standard models. Check logo quality, workmanship, charging performance, and compatibility. Only approve samples after thorough testing. During mass production, our 4-stage QC process ensures consistent quality: IQC (incoming component inspection), IPQC (in-process monitoring), FQC (final functional test), OQC (outgoing quality check). 100% of units pass a 4-hour aging test before shipping.

Stage 1 —IQC (Incoming Quality Control): All raw materials and components inspected before entering production. Stage 2 —IPQC (In-Process Quality Control): Real-time monitoring during SMT assembly, soldering, and assembly. Stage 3 —FQC (Final Quality Control): 100% functional testing of every unit —charging speed, port output, safety protection, and compatibility. Stage 4 —OQC (Outgoing Quality Control): Final appearance check, packaging verification, and quantity confirmation before shipping.

Standard 12-month warranty on all OEM/ODM products, extendable to 24 months for qualified partners. Defect rate target: under 1%. For confirmed manufacturing defects, we provide replacement units or credit on your next order within 15 business days. Our after-sales team responds to quality issues within 24 hours. Long-term partners receive priority RMA processing.

Certifications

Required: CE marking (electrical safety + EMC), RoHS (hazardous substances), WEEE registration. For products with lithium batteries: UN38.3 (transport safety) and battery directive compliance. Our products come with CE and RoHS documentation by default. We assist with obtaining additional certifications for your target EU markets.

Required: FCC (electromagnetic interference), UL 62368-1 (safety standard for power supplies). Recommended: ETL or cUL for retail distribution. For power banks: UN38.3 for battery transport compliance. We provide FCC testing documentation with every OEM order and can coordinate UL/ETL certification through our partner testing labs. Certification lead time: 2-4 weeks.

Yes. We handle certifications as part of our OEM/ODM service. Standard certifications (CE, FCC, RoHS, UN38.3) are included with every order. For market-specific certifications (UL for US, PSE for Japan, KC for Korea, SAA for Australia), we coordinate with accredited third-party labs and manage the application process. Certification costs are quoted separately for project-specific requirements.

Product Technology

GaN (Gallium Nitride) is a semiconductor material that replaces traditional silicon in chargers. Benefits: 40% smaller size, 30% better heat dissipation, higher efficiency (less energy wasted as heat), and support for higher power output in compact form factors. WOWOHCOOL uses 5th generation GaN (GaN V) in our latest charger lines. Browse our GaN charger lineup.

Semi-solid-state battery technology replaces the liquid electrolyte in traditional lithium-ion batteries with a gel-like solid electrolyte. Benefits: 50% thinner profile, 30% higher energy density, significantly reduced fire risk, and longer cycle life (500+ cycles). WOWOHCOOL's semi-solid-state power bank line was featured at CES 2026. Explore semi-solid-state power banks.

Qi2 is the latest wireless charging standard from the Wireless Power Consortium. Key feature: MPP (Magnetic Power Profile) uses N52H neodymium magnets for perfect alignment every time, ensuring 15W fast charging without the "searching for the sweet spot" frustration. Fully backwards compatible with existing Qi devices. Supports iPhone MagSafe and Android devices. View Qi2 wireless chargers.

Business Operations

We take IP protection seriously. We sign NDAs and NNN (Non-Use, Non-Disclosure, Non-Circumvention) agreements. For OEM clients: your design files and specifications remain your property. For ODM: mutually agreed IP ownership terms in the contract. Molds and tooling owned by you remain under your control. We do not share client designs with third parties. For critical IP, we recommend design patent registration in China as additional protection.

Chinese New Year (CNY) typically falls in January or February. Production closes for 10-14 days. We recommend placing orders 6-8 weeks before CNY to ensure production completion before the holiday. Post-CNY, production ramps up over 1-2 weeks. Q1 capacity is tight as factories fulfill pre-CNY backlog. We monitor the official CNY schedule annually and advise clients in advance.
