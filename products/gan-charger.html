GaN Charger FAQ

Essential for OEM/ODM partnerships

01

What is the MOQ for OEM GaN charger orders?

Our MOQ is 500 units per SKU for standard GaN V models. Custom ODM projects with unique housing or multi-port configurations require 1000+ units. All GaN chargers benefit from our automated production lines, ensuring consistent quality across high-volume orders.

02

What fast charging protocols are supported?

Our GaN chargers support PD 3.1 (up to 140W), PPS, QC 5.0, SCP, and AFC. Universal compatibility from iPhone 15 to MacBook Pro 16 and Samsung Galaxy devices.

03

Are GaN chargers safe?

Yes. GaN chargers generate less heat than silicon chargers, reducing fire risks. They include advanced over-voltage/current protection and thermal monitoring.

04

What power options are available?

20W-240W range. Popular: 20W-30W (phone), 45W-65W (laptop), 100W-140W (high-performance). All use latest GaN V technology.

05

Can you customize the design?

Yes. Our OEM/ODM service includes custom CMF, logo printing, packaging design, and port configuration. GaN-specific options include foldable AC pins, retractable cables, integrated power strip designs, and multi-port desktop stations with GaN V technology.

06

What efficiency advantages does GaN V offer?

GaN V technology delivers 92-95% power efficiency compared to 85-88% for traditional silicon chargers. This means 40% smaller size, 30% less heat generation, and compliance with DOE Level VI / EU ErP efficiency standards. Ideal for brands targeting premium market segments.
