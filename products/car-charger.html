Car Charger FAQ

Compatibility & Safety Insights

01

Does your car charger support 45W for Samsung S24 Ultra?

Yes. Our car chargers support PPS (Programmable Power Supply), providing the full 45W 'Super Fast Charging 2.0' required for Samsung flagship devices including Galaxy S24 Ultra, S23, and Note series.

02

Can these be used in 24V trucks as well as 12V cars?

Absolutely. Our internal DC-DC buck-boost converters are designed for a wide input range of 12V-24V, making them suitable for everything from family sedans to commercial trucks and heavy-duty vehicles.

03

Do your car chargers support PD 3.1 for laptop charging?

Yes. Our flagship models support PD 3.1 with up to 140W output, compatible with MacBook Pro, Dell XPS, ThinkPad, and other USB-C laptops. The WOC24 140W model features dual PD ports delivering 65W + 30W simultaneously.

04

Will high-power charging (100W) overheat the device?

No. Our GaN-powered car adapters handle up to 140W output while maintaining a cool exterior. We use advanced thermal management with multiple air vents, intelligent thermal throttling, and premium materials like aluminum alloy or V0 fire-retardant PC shells.

05

What safety certifications do your car chargers have?

All models are fully compliant with E-Mark, CE, FCC, and RoHS for global automotive markets. We also conduct 24-hour salt spray testing for anti-corrosion and 3000+ plug/unplug cycle tests to ensure long-term durability.
