Power Bank FAQ

Essential for OEM/ODM partnerships

01

What is the MOQ for OEM power bank orders?

Our MOQ is 500 units per SKU for standard models. Custom ODM projects with unique battery cell configurations (such as semi-solid-state) require 1000+ units. We provide flexible MOQ options for startup brands—contact us to discuss your volume requirements.

02

What capacity options do you offer?

We manufacture from 5,000mAh to 40,000mAh. Popular options: 10,000mAh (slim), 15,000mAh (hybrid), 20,000mAh-27,000mAh (high-capacity). All use Grade A Li-polymer cells.

03

What certifications do your power banks have?

All power banks are CE, FCC, RoHS certified and UN38.3 tested for safe air transport. We provide complete documentation including MSDS reports.

04

Can you customize the design?

Yes. Our OEM/ODM service includes custom CMF, logo printing, packaging design, and capacity configuration. Power bank-specific options include TFT display integration, wireless charging pads, 2-in-1 hybrid designs (wall charger + power bank), and semi-solid-state battery technology.

05

What is the production lead time?

Standard OEM: 25-35 days after sample approval. Custom ODM: 45-60 days. Express tooling and rapid prototyping services available.

06

Do you offer semi-solid-state battery power banks?

Yes. We offer semi-solid-state battery power banks - the CES 2026 award-winning technology. 50% thinner, 30% higher energy density, safer than Li-Po. Available in 5,000mAh-10,000mAh with Qi2.2 wireless charging support.
