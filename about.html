About Wowohcool

Dongyi Technology Co., Ltd. was established in 2016 and is a leading supplier of consumer electronic products. mainly engaged in mobile power supply, wireless chargers, automotive electronics, Bluetooth, mobile phone accessories, etc.; it is the preferred supplier of OEM/ODM for many well-known brands at home and abroad.
The factory covers an area of 1500 square meters, has over 150 employees, 8 modern manual production lines, and 1 intelligent automatic production line. With 10 sets of CNC machine tools, injection molding machines, die-casting machines, punching machines, etc., we can engage in mass order production and timely delivery.
