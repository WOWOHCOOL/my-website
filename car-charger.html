Car Charger FAQ

Quality & Safety Assurance

01

Is it compatible with 24V trucks and heavy vehicles?

Yes. All our car chargers support wide-voltage input (12V-24V), making them suitable for everything from family sedans to large commercial trucks.

02

How do you ensure the retractable cable's durability?

We use high-elasticity TPE materials and a reinforced internal spring mechanism. Each batch undergoes a 10,000-cycle pull-and-retract test in our QC lab.

03

Will high-power charging (100W) overheat the device?

No. Our high-power models feature multiple air vents and intelligent thermal throttling. The shell is made of heat-resistant PC/ABS or aluminum to ensure optimal cooling.
