Power Solutions FAQ

Essential information for OEM/ODM partnerships

01

How does the 2-in-1 Hybrid Charger manage heat?

We use advanced Thermal Conductivity Silica and a partitioned internal layout to isolate the GaN heat source from the battery cells, ensuring safe operation in both Wall Mode and Battery Mode.

02

Do you support custom capacity for Power Banks?

Yes. We offer 5,000mAh to 40,000mAh configurations with custom branding, CMF, and packaging for OEM/ODM orders. Grade A Li-polymer cells guarantee 500+ charge cycles.

03

What fast charging protocols do your GaN chargers support?

Our GaN chargers support PD 3.1 (up to 140W), PPS, QC 5.0, SCP, and AFC. This ensures universal compatibility from iPhone 15 to MacBook Pro 16 and Samsung Galaxy devices.

04

Can we customize built-in cable types and materials?

Yes. We offer full OEM configuration. Choose from Lightning, Type-C, or Micro-USB connectors with premium braided or liquid silicone finishes. We also offer foldable AC pins for travel-friendly designs.

05

Do you handle international battery shipping?

Yes. We provide all UN38.3, MSDS, and Sea/Air certificates required for Class 9 Dangerous Goods clearance. Our logistics team handles documentation for all major markets.
